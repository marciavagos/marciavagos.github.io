Biography

I have an Integrated Masters degree in Bioengineering, with a specialization in Biomedical Engineering.

I have recently obtained a PhD degree from the University of Oslo (Norway), undertaken at Simula Research Laboratory, within computational modelling of cardiac electrophysiology.

Previously, I worked at the Forschungszentrum Informatik, in Germany, where I developed a 3D model of the human eye, and simulated retinal damage driven by laser irradiation.
